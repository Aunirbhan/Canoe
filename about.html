General Overview

Welcome to the NYU Concrete Canoe team! We are an interdisciplinary group of students dedicated to designing, constructing, and racing a canoe made entirely out of concrete. This project tests our knowledge in civil engineering, materials science, and project management.

Our Subteams

Our team is divided into specialized subteams, ensuring all aspects of the competition are covered.

Mix Design

Developing the perfect concrete mixture.

Hull Design

Modeling and optimizing the canoe's shape.

Construction

Building the mold and pouring the canoe.

Aesthetics

Applying the final finish and graphics.

Marketting

Organizing social events, finding sponsors, and maintaining the teams media presence.

Special Projects

Responsible for miscellaneous tasks, often with a heavy interdisciplinary focus.
